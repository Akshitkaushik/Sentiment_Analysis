Here’s the corrected version of your points with numbering:

1. RPA will receive the Input report from ---- [TBD].

2. Login to EASY and select the Database as given in the input report.

3. Enter code as “CHGR” and hit “Enter.”

4. Insert Plan Number -> Press [Enter] then Press F12.
   - Check if a pop-up appears, then click on the OK button.

5. Select "Change group account recovery information," then press Enter.

6. Since the Rule is not on the plan:
   - Search for the Rule ID row by row in the RULE ID column. 
   - If the Rule ID is not found, insert a new row using Ctrl+F12 or the down arrow key.
   - Check if a pop-up appears, then click OK.
   - Insert the Rule ID from the spreadsheet in the [RULE ID column], then hit Enter.
   - Select the Rule ID from the below screen, then click OK.

7. Tab over to the effective date and insert the effective date from the spreadsheet in the [EFT DATE (if new rule added) column].

8. Press Tab [4 times] to reach the statement label column and insert the STATEMENT LABEL from the spreadsheet in the [STATEMENT LABEL column].

9. Press F6 to save.

10. Press F12 once to bring up the next screen.

11. Press F12 again to bring you to the next screen, which lands you on the description screen.
    - Insert DESCRIPTION from the spreadsheet in the [DESCRIPTION column].

12. Press F12 three times to bring you to the amount of Fee.
    - Insert the amount from the spreadsheet in the [FEE AMOUNT column].
    - Press F6 to save.

13. If the CALC Y value exists in the spreadsheet, the bot will perform the same action as filling the CALC Y value on the [AFREALLOC rule not on plan].

14. Press F11 four times to bring you back to this screen, then press the down arrow key once to VIEW PAYEE ACCOUNT INFO, then press F12.

15. On the payment receiver type, press SHIFT+F1 to bring up a drop-down, pick "Plan holding account," and hit Enter.

16. Hit Enter again to move to the share PCT and enter 100.

17. Enter the effective date by inserting the date from the spreadsheet in the [EFT DATE (if new rule added) column].

18. Press Enter twice to land on the Money Type.

19. Press SHIFT+F1 to bring up a drop-down menu. At this point, pick the UPA2 option.

20. If there are more than one UPA2 choices, take a screenshot and press F3 to go to the next record.

21. If there is only one UPA2 choice, select it.

22. Hit Enter, and it will fill in the information from the choice you made.

23. Press Enter five times to reach ARS NAME. You may get a pop-up; press Enter to bypass it.

24. Once on ARS NAME, press SHIFT+F1 and pick "FEE_REPORT_TRF," hit Enter, then press F6 to save.

25. Press F11, then press Ctrl+F9 to bring you to the assessment row section.

26. Insert the begin, end, and schedule dates from the spreadsheet [BEGIN, END, SCHE DATE columns]. The schedule date must end with 00:00.

27. Hit Enter to move to REQUESTED AMOUNT (the system at this point should insert a date into the EFFECTIVE column—nothing to insert here).
    - Press F6 to save, then press F3 twice to return to CHGR.

28. Click OK and press F3 twice to exit the plan and return to CHGR to start on the next plan in the list.
